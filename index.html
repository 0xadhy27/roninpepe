About Ronin

$ROPE Ronin Pepe (RPEPE) is a cryptocurrency that operates on the Binance Smart Chain (BSC),
which is a blockchain network compatible with Ethereum.
Ronin Pepe is a meme token inspired by the popular Pepe the Frog character and the Ronin theme of samurai warriors without masters. Ronin Pepe can be traded on PancakeSwap, a decentralized exchange on BSC, for Wrapped BNB (WBNB) tokens.

Ronin Pepe is one of the many meme tokens that have emerged on BSC, following the trend of Dogecoin and Shiba Inu.
